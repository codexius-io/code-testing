FAQ

Questions before getting started?

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about working with Codexius.

How long does a website take? +

Most small-business websites take several days to a few weeks depending on page count, features, content readiness, and revision needs.

Can you redesign my existing website? +

Yes. We can improve the design, mobile layout, navigation, messaging, speed, and overall customer experience.

Will my website work on phones? +

Yes. Every Codexius website is designed for phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop screens.

Can you connect my domain? +

Yes. We can help configure your DNS records, connect the website, and enable secure HTTPS.

Can forms send to my business email? +

Yes. Form submissions can be delivered directly to support@codexius.io or another preferred inbox.
